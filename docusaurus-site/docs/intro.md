## What is Physical AI?

Physical AI extends beyond digital spaces into the physical world, representing a paradigm shift from traditional robotics and pure software AI. These systems:

- **Operate in reality** while comprehending physical laws
- **Integrate sensing and actuation** with learned models
- **Reason about physical constraints** and long-horizon tasks
- **Enable natural human-robot interaction** through multi-modal perception
